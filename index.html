Research interests

My research interests expand from machine learning, neural networks, deep learning and optimization methods to design and analysis of algorithms and bioinformatics. I find machine learning and deep neural networks amazing and I am curious to investigate the theoretical aspects and limitations of these methods with an optimization and statistical approach.

Designing and analysis of algorithms have always been compelling for me. As an example, randomized algorithms are extremely exciting and fun to study in theory.

Lately, I have been most curious about bioinformatics and how a statistical, algorithmic approach can be groundbreaking in analyzing and decoding DNA sequences as well as the challenges in specified data structures to represent and query such sequences efficiently.
